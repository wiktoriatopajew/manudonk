## 1. REFUND POLICY
*(Shopify Admin → Settings → Policies → Refund policy)*

**Digital Products — No Return Policy**

All products sold by ManualBear are digital downloads (PDF workshop manuals). Because digital products are delivered instantly and cannot be "returned," all sales are final.

**When we issue a refund:**
We will provide a full refund only if:
- The file you received is corrupt or unreadable, AND
- Our support team is unable to resolve the issue within 48 hours of your report.

To request a refund, contact us at support@manualbear.com within **10 days of purchase** and include your order number and a description of the problem.

**No refunds for:**
- Incorrect vehicle purchased by the customer
- Change of mind after download
- Compatibility issues with third-party software not listed in the product description
- Sale items and gift cards

**EU Customers — Right of Withdrawal:**
Under EU Directive 2011/83/EU, consumers in the European Union have a 14-day right of withdrawal. However, this right does not apply to digital content (including PDF downloads) once the download has begun, provided the customer gave explicit prior consent and acknowledged the loss of the withdrawal right at checkout.

By completing your purchase and initiating the download, you confirm your consent and acknowledge that your right of withdrawal is waived for this transaction.

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within **10 business days**.
