About Manual Donkey

Your Trusted Digital Archive for Technical Documentation

Our Mission

Manual Donkey was founded in 2024 with a clear purpose: to support the longevity of technology and promote sustainability by providing easy access to essential technical documentation. We understand the frustration of losing a manual or struggling to find guides for legacy equipment. Our service bridges this gap by offering a curated digital library of over 1,000 professional user manuals and service guides, delivered instantly to support your repair and maintenance needs.

Who We Are

We are a legally registered business based in Płock, Poland. Operating transparently within the European Union, we pride ourselves on regulatory compliance and professional service. Manual Donkey acts as a specialized provider, focusing on the retrieval, restoration, and digital organization of technical documents to help users worldwide maintain their equipment.
