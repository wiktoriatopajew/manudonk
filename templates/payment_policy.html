Payment Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

We accept the following secure payment methods through Stripe:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover.
  • Digital Wallets: Google Pay, Apple Pay.
  • Local Payments: BLIK (Poland).

All transactions are encrypted with SSL. Your payment data is never stored on our servers.

Currency

All prices on ManualBear are listed in USD (US Dollars). The price displayed on the product page and at checkout is the final price you will be charged. There are no hidden fees, taxes, or surcharges added at checkout.

If your payment method is denominated in a different currency, your bank or card issuer may apply their own exchange rate and/or foreign transaction fees. ManualBear has no control over these third-party charges.

Payment Security

All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor — the highest level of security certification available. We never have access to your full card number or payment credentials.

Contact

If you have any questions about payments, please contact us at support@manualbear.com or call +48 516 531 395.
