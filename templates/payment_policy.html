Payment Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

1. Accepted Payment Methods

Manual Donkey accepts payments primarily through Stripe, a global leader in secure online payment processing. We support the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover.
  • Digital Wallets: Apple Pay, Google Pay.
  • Local Methods: BLIK (for customers in Poland).

2. Secure Transactions

Security is our top priority. All transactions are securely processed by Stripe.

  • SSL Encryption: Our entire website is secured with industry-standard SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption to protect your data.
  • PCI-DSS Compliance: We do not store your credit card details on our servers. All payment data is handled directly by Stripe's PCI-DSS Service Provider Level 1 certified systems.

3. Payment Terms

Immediate Billing: Your payment method will be charged at the time of purchase. Access to your digital download is granted immediately upon successful payment confirmation.

Currency: All prices are displayed and charged in United States Dollars (USD) unless otherwise noted by your local payment provider.

4. Billing Descriptor

Charges on your bank statement will appear as MANUALDONKEY or MANUALDONKEY.COM.

5. Tax Information

For customers within the European Union, VAT (Value Added Tax) is included in the price where applicable, in accordance with EU regulations for digital goods.
