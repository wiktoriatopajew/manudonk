Delivery Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

ManualBear sells digital products only. Every item is a PDF document delivered electronically. There is no physical shipping, and there are no shipping costs.

1. How You Receive Your Order

As soon as your payment is confirmed, a download link for your PDF manual is sent automatically to the email address you used at checkout. The same link is also shown on the order confirmation page.

2. Delivery Time

  • Delivery method: Email / instant download.
  • Delivery time: Instant — typically within 1–5 minutes of a successful payment.
  • Availability: Worldwide, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
  • Cost: $0.00 — there are no delivery or shipping charges.

3. Didn't Receive Your Download?

If your link has not arrived within a few minutes, please:

  • Check your spam / junk / promotions folder.
  • Make sure the email address entered at checkout was correct.
  • Contact us at support@manualbear.com with your order number — we will re-send your download right away.

4. Re-Downloading Your Files

Your download remains available through the link we email you. If you ever lose access, contact us and we will re-issue it free of charge.

5. Need Help?

For any question about delivery, email support@manualbear.com or call +48 516 531 395. We typically reply within 24 hours on business days.
