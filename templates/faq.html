📄 Ordering & Delivery

How do I place an order?

Search for your product, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can checkout as a guest or create an account to track your orders. Once your payment is confirmed, the download is delivered to your email automatically.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure payment gateway. All transactions are fully encrypted.

How are products delivered?

All products are digital (PDF). As soon as your payment is confirmed, the download is delivered to your email automatically — there is nothing to ship and no shipping cost. See our Delivery Policy for details.

How quickly will I get my manual?

Instantly. The download link is sent to your email within moments of a successful payment, so you can access your manual straight away, 24/7.

📖 Product Information

What format are the manuals in?

All manuals are delivered as PDF files (digital download) sent instantly to your email. PDFs open on any computer, tablet or phone and can be printed at home.

What kind of documentation do you provide?

We provide high-quality technical documentation and service information for a wide range of vehicles and equipment. Our archive includes workshop manuals, repair guides, and maintenance instructions carefully compiled and verified for accuracy.

Can I download my manual again later?

Yes. Your manual is available via the download link sent to your email. If you ever lose access, just contact us at support@manualbear.com with your order number and we will re-send it.

🔄 Returns & Refunds

What is your refund policy?

Our products are digital. If a file is corrupted, will not open, or is not delivered, contact us at support@manualbear.com and we will re-send it or issue a full refund. Because downloads are delivered instantly, the EU right of withdrawal is waived once the download begins (with your consent). For full details, see our Return & Refund Policy.

Are there any hidden or extra fees?

No. The price shown at checkout is the final price — there are no shipping charges, restocking fees or surcharges. Approved refunds are issued in full.

How long does a refund take?

Once your refund is approved, we process it immediately. It typically takes 5–7 working days for your bank to reflect the credit on your original payment method.

What if my file won't open or is incomplete?

If your PDF is corrupted or incomplete, please contact us immediately at support@manualbear.com with your order number. We will send a fresh copy free of charge.
