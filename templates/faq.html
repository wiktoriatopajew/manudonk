Find quick answers to the most common questions about our products and services.

📦 Ordering & Shipping

How do I place an order?

Search for your product, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can checkout as a guest or create an account to track your orders. Once your payment is confirmed, we prepare your order for shipment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure payment gateway. All transactions are fully encrypted.

How are products shipped?

We supply workshop manuals and technical guides on physical media (USB sticks or DVDs). These are dispatched from our office in Płock, Poland, via registered mail or courier service to ensure safe delivery. For full details, see our Shipping & Delivery Policy.

Do you provide tracking for my order?

Yes. Once your package is dispatched, we will email you a tracking number so you can monitor your delivery status.

💿 Product Information

What format are the manuals in?

Our products are delivered on physical media (USB flash drives or DVDs). Simply insert the drive into your computer to access your technical manuals and documentation.

Are these original manufacturer manuals?

Yes. We provide high-quality service guides and technical documentation sourced from original manufacturers and authorized distributors.

What if I need a digital copy?

While we specialize in physical media for long-term storage and reliability, our USB/DVD products are "plug-and-play," meaning you can easily access the files on any device with a USB port or DVD drive.

🔄 Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy for physical products?

If you wish to return a physical item (USB/DVD), please notify us within 14 days of delivery. The item must be in its original, unused condition. Please contact us at support@manualdonkey.com before sending any items back. For full details, see our Return & Refund Policy.

Are there any restocking fees?

No. We do not charge any restocking fees for returned items. You will receive a full refund of the product price.

How long does a refund take?

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we process the refund immediately. It typically takes 5–7 working days for your bank to reflect the credit on your original payment method.

What if my product arrives damaged?

If your USB stick or DVD arrives defective or damaged, please contact us immediately at support@manualdonkey.com with your order number. We will provide a free replacement.
